Solution 9.8.1.0.15.1

\begin{align*} \fe{\fd{f}}{x}&=\lzoo{x}{\fe{\cos^2}{x}+\fe{\sin}{x}}\\ &=2\fe{\cos}{x}\cdot-\fe{\sin}{x}+\fe{\cos}{x}\\ &=\fe{\cos}{x}\left(-2\fe{\sin}{x}+1\right) \end{align*}
The domain of \(f\) has been restricted to \(\cinterval{0}{2\pi}\). Over \(\cinterval{0}{2\pi}\), \(\fe{\cos}{x}=0\) at \(\frac{\pi}{2}\) and \(\frac{3\pi}{2}\) and \(\fe{\sin}{x}=\frac{1}{2}\) at \(\frac{\pi}{6}\) and \(\frac{5\pi}{6}\). So over the restricted domain, \(\fe{\fd{f}}{x}=0\) at \(\frac{\pi}{2}\), \(\frac{3\pi}{2}\), \(\frac{\pi}{6}\), and \(\frac{5\pi}{6}\). \(\fd{f}\) is never undefined. So the critical numbers of \(f\) are \(\frac{\pi}{6}\), \(\frac{\pi}{2}\), \(\frac{5\pi}{6}\), and \(\frac{3\pi}{2}\).
