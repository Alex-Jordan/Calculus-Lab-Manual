Solution 3.5.1.0.12.1

At the pace of \(300\) ft⁄min, Carl's heart rate changes relative to his pace at a rate of \(0.02\,\frac{\sfrac{\text{beats}}{\text{min}}}{\sfrac{\text{ft}}{\text{min}}}\). So, if Carl picks up his pace from \(300\) ft⁄min to \(301\) ft⁄min we'd expect his heart rate to increase to about \(84.02\,\frac{\text{beats}}{\text{min}}\). Conversely, if Carl decreases his pace from \(300\) ft⁄min to \(299\) ft⁄min we'd expect his heart rate to decrease to about \(83.98\,\frac{\text{beats}}{\text{min}}\).
