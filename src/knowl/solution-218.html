Solution 9.8.1.0.2.1

\begin{align*} \lzoo{t}{\fe{\cosh}{t}}&=\lzoo{t}{\frac{e^{t}+e^{-t}}{2}}&\lzoo{t}{\fe{\sinh}{t}}&=\lzoo{t}{\frac{e^{t}-e^{-t}}{2}}\\ &=\frac{e^{t}-e^{-t}}{2}&&=\frac{e^{t}+e^{-t}}{2}\\ &=\fe{\sinh}{t}&&=\fe{\cosh}{t} \end{align*}
It follows that \(\lzon{2}{t}{\fe{\cosh}{t}}=\fe{\cosh}{t}\) and \(\lzon{2}{t}{\fe{\sinh}{t}}=\fe{\sinh}{t}\).
