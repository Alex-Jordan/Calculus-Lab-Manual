Solution 5.12.1.0.21.1

\begin{align*} \fe{f}{x}&=\frac{x^3+x^2}{x}\\ &=x^2+x& x\neq0\\ \fe{\fd{f}}{x}&=2x+1& x\neq0 \end{align*}
\(\fe{f}{5}=30\) and \(\fe{\fd{f}}{5}=11\), so the tangent line to \(f\) at \(5\) passes through the point \(\point{5}{30}\) and has a slope of \(11\). The equation of this line is \(y=11(x-5)+30\).
