Solution 3.5.1.0.4.1

If \(\fe{f}{x}=\fe{\sin}{x}\), then

\begin{align*} \fe{\fd{f}}{x}&=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\fe{f}{x+h}-\fe{f}{x}}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\fe{\sin}{x+h}-\fe{\sin}{x}}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\fe{\sin}{x}\fe{\cos}{h}+\fe{\cos}{x}\fe{\sin}{h}-\fe{\sin}{x}}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\fe{\sin}{x}\left(\fe{\cos}{h}-1\right)+\fe{\cos}{x}\fe{\sin}{h}}{h}\\ &=\fe{\sin}{x}\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\fe{\cos}{h}-1}{h}+\fe{\cos}{x}\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\sin{h}}{h}\\ &=\fe{\sin}{x}\cdot0+\fe{\cos}{x}\cdot1\\ &=\fe{\cos}{x} \end{align*}
