Solution 5.12.1.0.31.1

\begin{align*} \fe{g}{t}&=3\fe{\sin}{t}+3\fe{\sin^2}{t}-5\\ \fe{g}{t}&=3\fe{\sin}{t}+3\fe{\sin}{t}\cdot\fe{\sin}{t}-5\\ \fe{\fd{g}}{t}&=3\fe{\cos}{t}+3\left(\lzoo{t}{\fe{\sin}{t}}\cdot\fe{\sin}{t}+\fe{\sin}{t}\cdot\lzoo{t}{\fe{\sin}{t}}\right)\\ \fe{\fd{g}}{t}&=3\fe{\cos}{t}+3\left(\fe{\cos}{t}\cdot\fe{\sin}{t}+\fe{\sin}{t}\cdot\fe{\cos}{t}\right)\\ \fe{\fd{g}}{t}&=3\fe{\cos}{t}+6\fe{\cos}{t}\fe{\sin}{t}\\ \fe{\fd{g}}{t}&=3\fe{\cos}{t}\left(1+2\fe{\sin}{t}\right) \end{align*}
So \(\fe{\fd{g}}{t}=0\) where \(\fe{\cos}{t}=0\) and where \(\fe{\sin}{t}=-\frac{1}{2}\). Over \(\cinterval{0}{2\pi}\), \(\fe{\cos}{t}=0\) at \(t=\frac{\pi}{2}\) and \(t=\frac{3\pi}{2}\). Over \(\cinterval{0}{2\pi}\), \(\fe{\sin}{t}=-\frac{1}{2}\) at \(t=\frac{7\pi}{6}\) and \(t=\frac{11\pi}{6}\). This agrees with the answer to ExerciseÂ 30.
