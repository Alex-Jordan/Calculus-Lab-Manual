Solution 1.4.1.0.1.1

The difference quotient for \(z\) is

\begin{align*} \frac{\fe{z}{x+h}-\fe{z}{x}}{h}&=\frac{\left[2+4(x+h)-(x+h)^2\right]-\left[2+4x-x^2\right]}{h}\\ &=\frac{2+4x+4h-x^2-2xh-h^2-2-4x+x^2}{h}\\ &=\frac{4h-2xh-h^2}{h}\\ &=\frac{h(4-2x-h)}{h}\\ &=4-2x-h\quad\text{for $h\neq0$} \end{align*}
