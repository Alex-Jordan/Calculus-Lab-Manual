Solution 1.4.1.0.10.1

\begin{align*} &\phantom{={}}\frac{\fe{k}{t+h}-\fe{k}{t}}{h}\\ &=\frac{\frac{(t+h-8)^2}{t+h}-\frac{(t-8)^2}{t}}{h}\\ &=\frac{\frac{t^2+th-8t+th+h^2-8h-8t-8h+64}{t+h}-\frac{t^2-8t-8t+64}{t}}{h}\\ &=\frac{\frac{t^2+2th-16t+h^2-16h+64}{t+h}-\frac{t^2-16t+64}{t}}{h}\frac{t(t+h)}{t(t+h)}\\ &=\frac{(t^2+2th-16t+h^2-16h+64)t-(t^2-16t+64)(t+h)}{ht(t+h)}\\ &=\frac{t^3+2t^2h-16t^2+th^2-16th+64t-t^3-t^2h+16t^2+16th-64t-64h}{ht(t+h)}\\ &=\frac{t^2h+th^2-64h}{ht(t+h)}\\ &=\frac{h(t^2+th-64)}{ht(t+h)}\\ &=\frac{t^2+th-64}{t(t+h)}\quad\text{for $h\neq0$} \end{align*}
