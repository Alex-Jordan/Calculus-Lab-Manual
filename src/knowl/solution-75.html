Solution 3.5.1.0.18.1

Twenty seconds after lift-off the space shuttle's elevation is increasing at a rate of \(0.074\) mi⁄s. So, we'd expect that \(19\) seconds into lift-off the elevation was about \(0.626\) mi and \(21\) seconds into lift-off the elevation will be about \(0.774\) mi.
